Frequently Asked Questions

Arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork. Wear comfortable clothing, avoid heavy meals before your session, and stay hydrated. Let Deanna know about any injuries, allergies, or areas of concern.

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you when possible.

Some insurance plans cover massage therapy with a doctor's referral. We can provide receipts for reimbursement. Please check with your insurance provider for coverage details.

Frequency depends on your goals. For stress relief, once a month is common. For injury recovery or chronic pain, weekly or bi-weekly sessions may be recommended initially. Deanna will create a personalized treatment plan.

My Approach

I believe in a client-centered approach, combining various techniques to address individual needs. Every session begins with a consultation to understand your concerns and goals. I integrate deep tissue, myofascial release, and aromatherapy to create a customized experience that promotes healing and relaxation.
